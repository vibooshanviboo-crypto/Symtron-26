About the Institution

Muthayammal Engineering College

Established in the year 2000 under the aegis of the Muthayammal Educational Trust, Muthayammal Engineering College (Autonomous) stands as a distinguished beacon of technical education in Tamil Nadu. Nestled in a serene, green campus in Rasipuram, Namakkal district, the institution has spent over two decades nurturing innovation, research capability and engineering excellence among both rural and urban youth.

Driven by its core mission to deliver industry-ready, value-based technical education, MEC serves as an ecosystem where academic rigor meets hands-on execution — preparing students to solve real-world problems with confidence and creativity.

Autonomous Status

Granted academic autonomy by the UGC, enabling a dynamic, industry-aligned curriculum that evolves with technology.

NAAC A++ Grade

Accredited by NAAC with the prestigious 'A++' Grade — a testament to sustained quality in teaching, research and governance.

Anna University Affiliation

Permanently affiliated with Anna University, Chennai, and approved by the AICTE, New Delhi, for engineering excellence.

58,000+ Volumes

A comprehensive digital library, high-speed campus-wide Wi-Fi and top-tier computational facilities empower seamless research.

About the Department of ECE

Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) at Muthayammal Engineering College was established in the milestone year 2000. Over more than two decades, the department has grown into a premium hub for technical education and frontier research, consistently shaping students into skilled innovators capable of meeting modern electronic industry standards.

The department’s primary objective is to cultivate deep technical acumen alongside hands-on engineering execution, paving the way for advancements in automation, hardware design and global communication technologies.

NBA Accredited B.E. ECE

The undergraduate program is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation, meeting rigorous industry-relevant benchmarks.

Anna University Research Centre

Recognized by Anna University, Chennai, as a collaborative centre for Ph.D. and M.S. (By Research) programs.

Texas Instruments Lab

A dedicated embedded systems ecosystem with industry-standard hardware and software for mastering firmware development.

Wipro Learning Centre

Mission 10X Technology Learning Centre providing hands-on infrastructure to scale programming and system architecture skills.

About the Department of EE-VLSI

Department of Electronics Engineering (VLSI Design & Technology)

The Department of Electronics Engineering (VLSI Design and Technology) is committed to developing skilled engineers in the field of semiconductor technology, integrated circuit (IC) design, embedded systems, and advanced electronic systems. The department provides a strong foundation in analog and digital VLSI design, CMOS technology, FPGA, ASIC, verification, and system-on-chip (SoC) design through a balanced curriculum, modern laboratories, and industry-oriented learning.

Students are encouraged to participate in research, technical symposiums, workshops, internships, and innovation projects that enhance their practical knowledge and problem-solving abilities. The department strives to prepare graduates who can contribute to the rapidly evolving semiconductor and electronics industry.

IC & System Design

In-depth expertise in analog and digital integrated circuit design using industry-standard EDA tools.

FPGA & ASIC Verification

Comprehensive training on programmable logic arrays and ASIC verification methodologies.

Semiconductor Tech

Hands-on learning of CMOS technology, design rules, and semiconductor physics.

System-on-Chip (SoC)

Designing complex SoC architectures incorporating processor cores, memory, and interfaces.

The Team

About SYMTRON & SILIZIUM Committee

The SYMTRON & SILIZIUM Committee is a dedicated team of faculty members and student coordinators from the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, Muthayammal Engineering College (Autonomous). The committee is responsible for planning, organizing, and executing SYMTRON & SILIZIUM, the National Level Technical Symposium.

With a commitment to professionalism, teamwork, and innovation, the committee ensures the successful conduct of technical events, workshops, paper presentations, project expos, and participant engagement. Every member works together to provide a well-organized, inspiring, and memorable experience for all participants.
